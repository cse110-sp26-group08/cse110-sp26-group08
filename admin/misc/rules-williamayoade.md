# Group Contract - Team 08

## Purpose, Objectives and Values

The purpose of this team and project is to learn effective ways of software development and the development cycle. Through this project we will learn how to and more importantly how not to work in teams to develop meaningful software. Below are broad objectives and values we must represent.

- We must respect each other and understand that no role is greater than another.
- We must understand that all of us come from different backgrounds and have different skills, and so we must help each other to improve on our weaknesses.
- As all of us are doing this for the first time, we should understand that mistakes will be made. These mistakes should be seen as experiences we learn from and not as failures of individuals who committed them.
- Our main objective is to have a good time developing meaningful software, grades will follow.

## Communication Rules

- Slack will be the platform for all communication and important outside conversations and decisions must be made public on slack.
- Decisions about individual tasks must also be communicated with everyone to avoid confusion later on.
- Reply to announcements and messages must be made within 24 hours. Use @ and select the person you are addressing to to notify them.

## Common Responsibilities and Practices

### Standup Meetings

- Every week the team will meet and everyone will discuss what tasks were completed the previous week and tasks that need to be completed for the next week.
- Everyone is expected to attend physically or through zoom meetings.
- People attending the meeting through zoom meetings must have their video turned on. If video cannot be turned on on select occasions a valid reason must be given.
- People attending zoom meetings must take part in communications through mic or chat.
- Meeting notes will be taken by the team leaders.

### Tasks

- Tasks will be assigned once the previous task is completed.
- Each task will be assigned an appropriate time for completion so, the assigned person must complete that task within those limits.
- If you are unable to complete a task, don’t wait till the last minute. Notify the team leads early so that the task can be reassigned. (We are all in the same team not competing)

### Coding Practices

- You can code using whatever tools allowed by the class but it must be documented.
- You must test your code and provide proof in github pull requests.

### Asking for Help

- As all of us do not have development experience, it is expected that we will face challenges. But instead of trying to solve them alone it is better to ask on slack as someone might help you out. (Don’t forget its a team project for a reason)
- Another resource is professor, TA or tutor office hours. Himir will be attending most of the office hours, so in case someone has a question they can relay it through other team members who attend office hours. But it is highly recommended that the person having the question themselves go to office hours.

## Conflict Resolution Strategies

- Everyone is expected to behave professionally and respect each other, even during conflicts keeping in mind the values in the first section.
- Incase of conflict between members of the group, the standup meeting should be used as an open discussion and a democratic decision will be made according to the discussion.
- If the conflict is not resolved during the meeting, instructional staff will be consulted and a final decision will be made.

## Non-Compliance Rules

- If any of the above rules are violated, and the violation causes the project completion to slow down, it will be noted and reported to the instructional staff.
- Incase of emergencies, both the violation and the cause of emergency will be reported to the instructional staff
