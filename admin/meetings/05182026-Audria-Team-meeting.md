## Takeaways and Summary

Key outcomes from the meeting:
* The team got a better understanding of the upcoming assignments and deadlines, especially the demo video and code review.
* Audria reminded the team that the class cares more about the development process, teamwork, communication, and decision-making than just the final product.
* The team was encouraged to avoid burnout and focus on building features that are realistic and functional.
* The discussion about a mobile version helped the team think more about responsiveness and what features would actually make sense on a phone.
* For the video update, the team should focus mostly on showing the project, explaining the pipeline, and talking about team progress and future plans. AI usage can be mentioned, but it should not be the main focus.
* During the demo, Audria liked how the team worked together to solve issues during the presentation.
* The team learned that not having a mobile version is okay as long as there is a good reason for the decision and it is documented properly.
* The team also clarified where frontend design decisions and infrastructure choices should be documented.
