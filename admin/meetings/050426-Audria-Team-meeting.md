Audria provided guidance on these topics to help the team proceed with confidence.

### Architectural Decisions and Documentation

**Important:** TA Audria emphasized that all architectural decisions made by the team—including framework selections, technology choices, database decisions, and system design patterns—should be formally documented in **Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)**.

**ADR Format:** Each significant decision should include:

- Decision rationale
- Alternatives considered
- Context and constraints
- Consequences and trade-offs

This documentation ensures that decision-making process is transparent and that future team members can understand the reasoning behind choices.
